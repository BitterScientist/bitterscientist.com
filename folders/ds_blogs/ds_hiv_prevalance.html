Global HIV Prevalence Levels

First Posted: 2-26-2020
Last Updated: 2-26-2020

Data:
The HIV prevalence data was obtained from an epidemiological study conducted by UNIAIDS (1, 2). This data consisted of the total (% of population ages 15-49) that was seropositive for HIV. Data excluded from this dashboard: years before 1990, countries missing many values, and non country instances. The latitude and longitude data for each country was obtained by webscraping data from google development tools (3). Data was merged using an inner join on country name.
