When do people normally die?¶

Since death is part of the probability we intend to estimate, we will need to get data that gives us: - The number of people that died in a given year - How old they were when they died What we will calculate from this data
- Distribution of ages at death - Normalize the numbers to the total number who dies to get a probability distribution of dying at a given age.
