Creating multiple small subplots with Python Matplotlib.

Background Info

Object-Oriented API: one of two methods used in matplotlib to render visualizations by using instances of classes.

Why is this important?
Unlike the state-based interface (covered in a different post) method for visualization, OO-API provides greater flexibility in customizing your visualizations.
