How to fall in love with Python List Comprehensions.

Background Info

List Comprehensions: Are concise and 'readable' one-line for loops that creates a new list from a sequence.
new_list = [final_expression for item in old_sequence].

Why is this important?
List comprehensions are flexible, can generally outperform a standard for loop, and are easier to read. Knowing how to assemble list comprehensions is an import pythonic skill to have under your belt.
